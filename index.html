Whiskey Flavor Profiles by arcs!

"86 malt whiskies are scored between 0-4 for 12 different taste categories including sweetness, smoky, nutty etc." Source data

This is a prototypical reiteration of Christopher Ingram's similar chart. However, that chart plots points around a circle and fills the created shape with color; this isn't good because, for example a 4 surrounded by two 1's will have very little area. Instead of points, I use arcs. This way, strong attributes have large area and vice versa. I also scale the color from white to a gold color, which should make comparisons a little easier.

Written with the D3.js library.
